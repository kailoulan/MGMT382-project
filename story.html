Welcome to Zayt: A Legacy of Innovation

Founded in 2005, Zayt emerged as a visionary leader in sustainable oil production. From humble beginnings in West Texas, we have grown into a global brand recognized for clean energy initiatives and ethical sourcing.

Milestones

  1. 2005 - Founded by energy entrepreneur Leila Karim.
  2. 2010 - Launched first carbon-neutral refinery.
  3. 2020 - Expanded to international markets in North Africa.

What Makes Zayt Different?

  • Eco-friendly refining technology
  • Community-first sourcing programs
  • Fair labor practices
